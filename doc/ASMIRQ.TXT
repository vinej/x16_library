=== ASMIRQ - assembly VSYNC interrupt example (ASMIRQ.FTH) ===

Shows a pure-assembly interrupt handler installed on the 60 Hz VERA VSYNC IRQ.
Load:  S" ASSEMBLER.FTH" INCLUDED  S" ASMIRQ.FTH" INCLUDED

  IRQ-DEMO   arm the assembly ISR - a counter advances in the interrupt
  IRQ-TEST   self-check that the ISR ran

Technique: a CODE word installed with  ['] MYCODE IRQ  becomes the per-frame
handler (0 IRQ disarms). The callback runs on its own stacks, must be short and
stack-neutral, and must save/restore any VERA address ports it touches.
See user guide Section 6.3.
