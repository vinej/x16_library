=== GAMEYM - "catch the dots" with FM audio (GAMEYM.FTH) ===

The GAME.FTH "catch the dots" game with its sound switched from the VERA PSG to
the YM2151 FM synth.  Load:  S" GAMEYM.FTH" INCLUDED   (core only)

  PLAY       start the game (arrow keys to move, Start to quit)
  SELFTEST   non-interactive single-frame check

Only the audio section differs from GAME.FTH - it uses FMINIT / FMINST /
FMNOTE / FMVOL (see HELP AUDIOFM). A demonstration of swapping a sound backend.
